Known issues

• Error during search and downloading of products returning an error related to the SSL library; this error is related to this OSGeo4W issue https://trac.osgeo.org/osgeo4w/ticket/715, a possible solution is to uninstall completely QGIS and install the latest QGIS version, although this didn't solve the issue for some users.
• Preprocessing of Landsat 8 and 9 of Collection 2 Level 2 is affected by an issue causing incorrect values of temperature. Please exclude the temperature bands from the preprocessing until this issue is solved.
• The values of ROI_size displayed in the Signature details are incorrect.
